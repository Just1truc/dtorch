dtorchvision

Description

Custom models for Image classification and generation are disponible here. There is pretrained and trainable models. They are all made with the dtorch package.

Tip

If you ever make a model that you are proud of, do not hesitate to send it to me via email so that I can make it disponible to everyone.

Datasets of images are also available here.
